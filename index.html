Does this mean that there are early forms of religion among apes as well? Just how intelligent are our closest relatives, and are morality and culture perhaps not exclusively human achievements after all? In this journey into the origins of the moral mind, well-known authorities such as Jane Goodall, Frans de Waal and Volker Sommer introduce us to a fascinating realm of scientific and philosophical exploration as they ask: Where does the human start and the animal end?
