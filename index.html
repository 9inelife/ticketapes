In the old world, Apes clades from different diversities and zones around the world experience their zonal social gatherings until a new innovative world was inspired by the metaverse.

All the leaders of the apes clades from each zones came together and had an agreement to form a common tribe among the apes clades called the JOES, where all apes clades (Joes) from different zones can fraternize with other apes and participate in all kinds of events and gathering even on the metaverse.

This new world was accessible with the help of a ticket governing the new era of apes clades and was given a common name TICKET APES.
