NFT Ticketing Industry

is a whole new world of endless possibilities and great experiences, ripe for massive disruption and Ticket Apes NFT puts the key to unlocking this world at your finger tips.

The Ticket Apes NFT(the Joes) is a unique collection of 1,111 digital collectibles (NFT's) flipping tickets and socializing on the Solana blockchain, each NFT is hand drawn featuring dozens of unique traits and rarities.
"Imagine a club where members get to socialize and participate in social gatherings like sports events, musical concerts, conferences and other live events(including events on the metaverse)".

WHY APE IN?

Owning a "Joe" will grant you exclusive access to the TicketDAO, which invariably gives you guaranteed access to selected exclusive events happening around the world.


THE TICKET DAO

The Ticket DAO is primarily developed to help members access tickets to important events which ordinarily would be difficult to access due to high demand or inflation on secondary market places. By partnering with event organisers and NFT/Digital ticket providers we will help community members gain early access to selected events.

Holding at least one NFT automatically makes you a stakeholder and a beneficiary of all our future ventures. Check roadmap for detail. We shall setup a DAO vault with a significant portion of funds raised from the mint and royalties.

Members suggest and vote on what event tickets to be acquire weekly, to be collected by members at 5% -10% discount, ticket holders can choose to attend the live event or resell tickets for profit. Also, Members vote on what projects to invest in and when to liquidate them. Re-invest the profits or withdraw and distribute them.
